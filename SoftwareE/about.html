The Vayam Foundation NGO


We are a non-profit organisation on a mission to fill the stomachs of all the people and to make sure that no one dies out of HUNGER. VAYAM means ‘we’ in Sanskrit and hence we need all your support in eradicating starvation in our country. The most alarming figure revealed is that approximately 4500 children die every day under the age of five years in the country due to hunger and malnutrition. Over 20 crore Indians sleep with an empty stomach every day. Therefore, our organisation was established, to seek your help to end hunger for children and their families. We request you to be a part of our organisation and help thousands by volunteering with us. It is a kind request to donate the excess food in your homes to those who need it .
